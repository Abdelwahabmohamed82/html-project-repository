A huge issue is the reduction in access to health services with people not attending clinics due to different levels of lockdown, and out of fear of infection. This will lead to an increase in deaths from preventable and treatable conditions. Also, hundreds of thousands of children under the age of one are not receiving vaccines due to the Covid-19 lockdown.
